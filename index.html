Promotions plugin

Our WordPress plugin for on-site promotions. Schedule a fundraising lightbox for a week, then auto-swap it for a signup pushdown, and tailor the content per page, all with no code. Amnesty International used it to add an upsell lightbox with an embedded EN form, none of which EN supports out of the box. Seven promotion types in all, and it is not only for donations.

Remember Me

Our cross-domain autofill cookie. One small file on the main domain lets any of an org's EN pages recognize a returning supporter and pre-fill their details, cutting friction on donation and advocacy forms. Live for WWF, PETA, and RAN, and being set up now for Food and Water Watch.
